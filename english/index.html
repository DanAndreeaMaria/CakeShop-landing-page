About us

“Sweet 3 Generation”: the name itself is a story. Three generations united by a passion for sweets, memories intertwined with flavors and dreams fulfilled. In the heart of the city of Deva, the boutique confectionery opens like a hidden treasure. Here, each cake has a story. Mom, with her skillful hands, shapes each layer of cream, and my son, with his big and curious eyes, watches each movement with fascination. Together, we create sweets that are not only delicious, but also full of emotion! Sweet 3 Generation is not just a business. It is a journey through time, where the tastes of childhood are intertwined with the passion to create something special. Every cake, every cookie, every macaron is a piece of our soul. And when the door of the confectionery opens, we feel like we are welcoming guests into our home. Guests who come to share the joy of a slice of cake or to celebrate important moments. Here, every smile counts, every story is precious. With an open heart and sweet smiles, we invite you, dear community from Deva, to step into our door at Sweet 3 Generation. Here, every cake is a story, and every snack brings a drop of joy into our lives and into your souls.
